Crypt of the CSharpDancer

AI for a network game

Project Description

This was a small project I made during my first year at the IUT de Dijon. The goal was to make a little artificial intelligence to play the game. The game was a server and my AI was a client. Then, as I finished it sooner than expected, I made a server myself, adding moving monsters to the game, making it a little more complex.
