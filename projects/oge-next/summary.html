OGE Next is a grade viewer for Windows, Linux and MacOS. It allows you to view your grades from the OGE website. The only reason why this app exists is because the website is not able to show your grade average before the very end of the semester so you can't see if you'll pass or not before it happens and that's dumb. So I made this so you can see your grade average at any time.
