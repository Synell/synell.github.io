Scorbut

Trophy Management Website

Project Description

This was a small project I made with 3 other persons during my second year at the IUT de Dijon. The goal was to make a website by following specifications given by a "client". Scorbut is a web application allowing users to earn trophies and points, in order to motivate them to contribute and participate in events or to push them to carry out actions within their student life. I was in charge of the events management, the routes, the dynamic display of pages, the style and the account system. The rest of my team was in charge of the database, the user management, the trophies management and we all contributed to the logic of the website.
